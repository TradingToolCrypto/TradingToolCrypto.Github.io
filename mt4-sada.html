MT4 - SADA Robot

The SADA Robot is the only MetaTrader 4 product that TradingToolCrypto offers because there is one thing that MT4 does so well, we haven't needed MT5. SADA stands for Semi-Automated DAX Algorithms and its a High Frequency intraday trading system optimized for the GE30.Cash DAX Indices instrument. We only run this algorithm on one particular brokerage (RoboForex) since they offer an exclusive commission discounts and fixed 0.50 USD spreads which can't be beat by any other Forex Brokerage. With these special trading conditions, we had in mind High Frequency Trading that could extract profits through out the trading hours of the German Stock Exchange session.

SADA is packed with 4 different trading algoritms with an User Interface to adjust all the parameters. We went the extra mile to integrate telegram messenger app to also adjust the settings on the fly. This is the perfect combination for any day trader on the go.

Strategy

4 Trading Algorithms

  • Dollar Cost Averaging
  • Scalping
  • Position Building
  • Hedging

The four different strategies prevent alpha decay by avoiding the pitfalls of most automated trading systems that repeat a pattern over and over again. Since the markets are constantly evolving, the strategies we select for the current environment will be different. The parameters within the strategies can be adjusted easily through the user-interface or telegram to prevent over-fitting and over optimization that plague most robots on the market.
