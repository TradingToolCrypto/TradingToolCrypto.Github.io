Crypto Charts Package

The crypto charting package for MetaTrader5 - MT5 includes real time updated orderbooks, tick data, and candle stick bars on all timeframes (M1-Monthly). All the Historical data from the exchanges can be downloaded into Metatrader for technical analysis and backtesting strategies within the built in Strategy Tester. The historical crypto chart data can also be exported from MetaTrader into CSV format for later storage or use.
